What does COULD mean?
COULD = past ability — things you were able to do
🏊
I could swim
🎵
She could sing
🏃
They could run
COULD is easy! 🎉
could
I / You / He / She / It / We / They
all use could — no changes!
No -s for He/She!  ✅ She could  ❌ She coulds
What verb comes after COULD?
could
+
base verb
(no -s, no -ing, no -ed)
✅ Correct
She could swim
He could run
Dogs could jump
I could play
❌ Wrong
She could swims
He could running
Dogs could jumped
I could played
People using COULD

🏊

🎵

🚲

Animals using COULD
could + base verb
🦁

🐦

🐬

🐘
The elephant could lift heavy logs.
