Put COULD at the very start!
Could you swim?
Could she sing?
Could they run fast?
Short Answers

Yes, she could.
Yes, they could.
No ❌
No, I couldn't.
No, she couldn't.
No, they couldn't.
Verb after COULD in questions
Could
+
subject
+
base verb
✅ Correct
Could you swim?
Could she sing?
Could he run?
Could they fly?
❌ Wrong
Could you swims?
Could she singing?
Could he runs?
Could they flew?
Same rule as always — base verb after Could!
