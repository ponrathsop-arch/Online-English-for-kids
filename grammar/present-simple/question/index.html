Quick Summary!
Do → I/You/We/They
Do you play?
Do they watch?
Do we go?
Does → He/She/It
Does he eat?
Does she go?
Does it work?
Remember!
After Do/Does → verb stays BASE form!
Does she like? (NOT likes)  |  Does he eat? (NOT eats)
