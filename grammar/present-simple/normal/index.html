I / You / We / They
The verb stays the SAME!
I eat breakfast at 7.
You go to school at 8.
We play games after school.
They watch TV at night.
He / She / It
The verb CHANGES! + s / es
He eats breakfast at 7.
He does his homework.
She goes to school at 8.
She watches TV at night.
