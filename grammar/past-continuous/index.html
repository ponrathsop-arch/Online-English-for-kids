What was happening in the past! ⏳

💡 When do we use it? To talk about something that was in progress at a moment in the past, or was interrupted by another action. e.g. "She was reading when the phone rang."

📖 Choose a form

✅
Normal Form
Positive statements about what was happening
I was sleeping at 9 pm.
They were playing outside.
Positive ❌
Negative Form
Say what was NOT happening in the past
He wasn't watching TV.
We weren't going out.
Negative ❓
Question Form
Ask about what someone was doing before
Were you sleeping?
Was she cooking?
