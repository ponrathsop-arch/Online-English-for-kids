Question Formula
Have/Has + subject + past participle + ?
Have / Has goes to the FRONT!
Have you ever eaten sushi?
Has she visited Paris?
Have they finished yet?
Have or Has first?
Have → I/You/We/They
Have you eaten yet?
Have they arrived?
Have we met before?
Has → He/She/It
Has she called?
Has he done it?
Has it stopped?
💬 Statement
She has visited Paris.
❓ Question
Has she visited Paris?
Have you ever...?
Have + subject + PP?
Have you ever eaten mango?
Have they finished their project?
Has she called you today?
Has he done his homework yet?
Short Answers!
Have you eaten sushi?
✅ Yes, I have. ❌ No, I haven't.
Has she visited London?
✅ Yes, she has. ❌ No, she hasn't.
Have they finished?
✅ Yes, they have. ❌ No, they haven't.
Quick Summary!
Have questions
Have you seen it?
Have we met?
Have they finished?
Has questions
Has she eaten?
Has he done it?
Has it stopped?
Short answers
Yes, I/we/they have. No, I/we/they haven't. Yes, he/she/it has. No, he/she/it hasn't.
