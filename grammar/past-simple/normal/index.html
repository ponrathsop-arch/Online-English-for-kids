Regular Verbs
Just add -ed! ✅
I walked to school yesterday.
She watched a movie last night.
They played football on Sunday.
He cleaned his room this morning.
Irregular Verbs
The verb CHANGES! 🔀
I ate pizza for lunch. (eat)
She went to the park. (go)
He saw a big dog! (see)
We had so much fun! (have)
Rule 1: Regular → add -ed
All subjects use the same form!
