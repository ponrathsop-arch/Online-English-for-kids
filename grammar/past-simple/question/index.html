The Formula
Did + Subject + base verb ?
Use Did for ALL subjects!
I / You / He / She / We / They → all use Did
Did she go to school? (NOT Did she went?)
Did he eat lunch? (NOT Did he ate?)
Short Answers ✅❌
Did you go to school?
✅ Yes, I did.
✅ Yes, she did.
❌ No, I didn't.
❌ No, she didn't.
After Did → always use BASE verb (not past form!)
Did + subject + verb? ❓
All subjects use Did!
Did you eat breakfast?
Did she go to the park?
Did he play football?
Did they watch a movie?
Short Answers ❓
Yes, did / No, didn't
Did Lisa walk? → Yes, she did. ✅
Did Tom study? → No, he didn't. ❌
Did you see it? → Yes, I did. ✅
Did they come? → No, they didn't. ❌
