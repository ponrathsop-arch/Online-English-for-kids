Negative Formula!
Subject + am/is/are + not + Adjective
Iam not sad.
He / She / Itis not angry.
You / We / Theyare not tired.
Short Forms!
Long form
am not
is not
are not
Short form
❌ no short form
isn't
aren't
I am not...
No short form for "I am not"!
I am not sad. I am happy!
I am not tired. I have lots of energy!
I am not angry anymore.
He / She isn't...
is not → isn't
He isn't bored. He is excited!
She isn't nervous about the test.
Tom isn't confused. He understands everything.
We / They aren't...
are not → aren't
We aren't tired yet. Let's keep playing!
They aren't sad. They are having fun!
We aren't overwhelmed anymore.
Quick Summary!
Long form
I am not sad
He is not tired
We are not angry
Short form
I am not (no short!)
He isn't tired
We aren't angry
Remember!
I → am not He/She/It → isn't You/We/They → aren't
