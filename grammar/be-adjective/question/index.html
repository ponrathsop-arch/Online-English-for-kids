Question Formula!
Am/Is/Are + Subject + Adjective ?
I → AmAm I happy?
He/She/It → IsIs she tired?
You/We/They → AreAre they excited?
How to Answer!
✅ Yes (I)Yes, I am.
❌ No (I)No, I am not.
✅ Yes (he/she)Yes, he/she is.
❌ No (he/she)No, he/she isn't.
✅ Yes (they)Yes, they are.
❌ No (they)No, they aren't.
Is he / she ...?
He/She/It → Is ...?
Is Mike happy? — Yes, he is.
Is she sad? — No, she isn't.
Is Tom tired? — Yes, he is.
Are you / they ...?
You/We/They → Are ...?
Are you excited? — Yes, I am!
Are they angry? — No, they aren't.
Are we ready? — Yes, we are!
