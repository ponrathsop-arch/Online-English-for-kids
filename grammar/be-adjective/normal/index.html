What is Be + Adjective?
We use it to describe...
Feelings
happy, sad,
tired, angry
Appearance
tall, short,
young, old
Qualities
smart, kind,
brave, funny
The Formula!
Subject + am / is / are + Adjective
Iam happy.
He / She / Itis tired.
You / We / Theyare excited.

😄 happy
feeling very good!
I am happy today.
She is happy because it's her birthday.
We are happy after the game.
😢 sad
feeling unhappy
He is sad because he lost his toy.
Sara is sad today.
They are sad when the movie ends.
😴 tired
needing sleep or rest
I am tired after school.
Tom is tired from working all day.
We are tired after the long trip.
🍔 hungry
wanting food!
I am hungry. Let's eat!
She is hungry after gym class.
They are hungry after school.
😠 angry
feeling very upset
He is angry about the broken rule.
Tom is angry when people are late.
They are angry at the bad news.
🎉 excited
very happy about something coming!
I am excited about the trip!
Lisa is excited about her new school.
They are excited for the party tonight.
😑 bored
not interested; nothing to do
He is bored on rainy days.
She is bored waiting at the bus stop.
We are bored without our phones.
😬 nervous
worried or a little scared
I am nervous before my test.
He is nervous about talking in front of the class.
They are nervous before the big match.
🦁 proud
happy about doing something well
I am proud of my drawing.
Lisa is proud of her first place medal.
We are proud of our team.
😕 confused
not understanding something
I am confused by this question.
He is confused about the directions.
They are confused by the new rule.
💤 exhausted
extremely tired — more than tired!
I am exhausted after running 5 km.
Tom is exhausted after working two jobs.
They are exhausted after the camping trip.
😱 terrified
extremely scared — more than scared!
She is terrified of spiders.
He is terrified of the dark.
We are terrified on the roller coaster.
🫣 embarrassed
shy or red-faced about a mistake
I am embarrassed when I make a mistake.
She is embarrassed after falling down.
They are embarrassed in front of everyone.
💪 determined
decided to do something, won't stop!
I am determined to win the race.
Tom is determined to finish the project.
They are determined to learn English.
🌊 overwhelmed
too much to handle at once!
I am overwhelmed with homework.
Sara is overwhelmed by the big project.
They are overwhelmed with new information.
